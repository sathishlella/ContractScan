# ContractScan AI — Startup One-Pager

## Problem
Every business signs contracts. Most small businesses and startups sign them without proper legal review because lawyers cost $300-600/hour. Dangerous clauses — unlimited liability, unilateral termination, broad IP assignment — go unnoticed until it's too late.

## Solution
ContractScan AI is an autonomous contract risk analysis agent. Paste any contract and get instant red flag detection, missing clause identification, risk scoring, and a negotiation playbook with suggested replacement language. No legal expertise required.

## How It Works
4-agent swarm architecture processes contracts in under 3 seconds:
1. Scout: Parses 60+ clause types, extracts entities (parties, dates, amounts)
2. Analyst: Runs 80+ red flag rules, detects missing protections, scores risk 0-100
3. Executor: Generates prioritized negotiation playbook with exact replacement language
4. Supervisor: Validates analysis quality, computes confidence score

## Differentiator
Core engine works with ZERO LLM dependency. No hallucinated legal advice. Deterministic, rule-based analysis that produces consistent, auditable results. LLM is optional enhancement only.
